About Dhalotaf Academy

Dhalotaf Academy is the first and only dedicated online tutorial in Ethiopia offering educational support in Afaan Oromoo for students from Grade 9 to Freshman level.

Our mission is to make learning accessible, relevant, and culturally connected for Oromo students nationwide.
